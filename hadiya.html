About us

The Hadiya Zone Science and Communication Technology Department in Ethiopia plays a crucial role in advancing technological education and innovation within the region. It focuses on enhancing digital literacy, promoting scientific research, and facilitating effective communication technologies. The department also collaborates with local institutions to foster sustainable development and bridge the digital divide.
